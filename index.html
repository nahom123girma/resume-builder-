Frequently Asked

Answers, before you even ask.

Don't see what you're looking for? Send us a note — we usually reply the same day.

Are ResumeFlow resumes ATS-friendly?
Yes — every template is tested against the major applicant tracking systems (Workday, Greenhouse, Lever, iCIMS, Taleo). We use clean structures, real text (no images of text), parseable section headers, and standard fonts so your resume reads correctly inside any system.
Can I upload my old resume?
Absolutely. Drop a PDF, DOCX, or TXT file in the Resume Builder or Cover Letter Builder. We'll extract your work history, education, and skills automatically — then suggest improvements before you generate anything new.
Can I download as PDF or Word?
Both. Every resume and cover letter exports cleanly to PDF or DOCX with one click. Pro and Business plans also support unlimited re-downloads after edits.
Does ResumeFlow create cover letters too?
Yes. Use the Cover Letter Builder to generate a tailored letter from your resume plus a job description. Choose a tone (warm, direct, energetic, minimal), edit inline, and export. Pro plans include unlimited generations.
Are my files private?
Yes. Files are encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest (AES-256). They're isolated per account, and we never train models on user content. You can permanently delete any document at any time from your dashboard.
Can I cancel anytime?
Anytime, no questions, no fees. Your plan stays active until the end of the current billing period, and you keep access to anything you've created.
How does the ATS Resume Checker work?
Paste any job description and we'll extract the keywords, weight them by importance, and compare against your resume. You get a 0–100 match score, a list of found and missing keywords, and ranked rewrites for the bullets that need them.
Do I get a free trial?
Free plans are free forever — 1 resume, 1 cover letter, and 3 daily PDF tool uses, no card required. Pro and Business include a 7-day free trial when you sign up.
What payment methods do you accept?
All major credit cards, debit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and bank transfers (Business plan). Payments are processed securely by Stripe — we never see or store card numbers.
Do you offer student or career-change discounts?
Yes. Verified students get 50% off the Pro plan, and we run a quarterly career-change grant program. Email hello@resumeflow.app with your situation and we'll point you to the right program.

Privacy Policy

Plain language, real protections.

This is the short version. The full legal text is below — but here's what actually matters.

Last updated: April 12, 2026

What we collect

To make ResumeFlow work, we collect the information you give us directly: your account details (name, email, password hash), your documents (resumes, cover letters, files you upload), and your generation history. We also collect basic usage data — pages visited, features used, errors encountered — so we can improve the product.

What we don't do

  • We don't sell your data. Ever. Not anonymized, not aggregated, not "as a benefit to partners."
  • We don't train AI models on your documents or chat history.
  • We don't share your resume with employers, recruiters, or third parties without your explicit consent.
  • We don't run ad-targeting cookies on the site.

How AI works here

When you generate a resume, cover letter, or use a PDF tool, your input is sent to our AI processing layer to produce the output. We use industry-leading providers and pass content over encrypted channels. Inputs are not retained for training and are deleted from processing logs within 30 days.

Your rights

You can export, edit, or permanently delete any document at any time from your dashboard. You can request a full copy of your data, or full account deletion, by emailing privacy@resumeflow.app — we honor requests within 30 days.

Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep you logged in and to remember your preferences. We use a minimal analytics cookie (anonymized, no cross-site tracking) to understand how the site is used. You can disable analytics from your account settings.

Data retention & deletion

Your account and documents are kept until you delete them or close your account. Once deleted, content is purged from our systems within 30 days, including backups within 90 days.

Children

ResumeFlow is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please email privacy@resumeflow.app.

Changes to this policy

We'll email you and post a banner in the product before any material change. The "last updated" date at the top will always reflect the current version.
